Prize & venue tags

Prize: 💰 cash = monetary prizes/gift cards · 🏅 recognition = presentation slots, certificates or trophies (no cash) · no prize listed = none stated (top teams are often still invited to present). Venue family colour-codes the source — MICCAI, ISBI, CVPR, RSNA — or Standalone/platform for community benchmarks not tied to a conference. The host (Kaggle, Codabench, Grand Challenge, Synapse, self-hosted) is where it runs online; note RSNA challenges are conference-affiliated but hosted on Kaggle.

How the GPU estimate works

A rough heuristic driven mainly by dimensionality and modality, plus whether the task implies foundation-model pretraining. 2D images fit an entry GPU (8–16 GB); gigapixel pathology ~16–24 GB; full-resolution 3D volumetric segmentation 24–48 GB; whole-body/multimodal 3D 40–48 GB; large-scale pretraining implies multi-GPU. Dataset size affects training time and disk more than peak VRAM. Treat every number as an order-of-magnitude starting point.
